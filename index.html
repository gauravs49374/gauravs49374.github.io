About Himalayan Vista Guest House

Nestled in the heart of the Himalayas! Sundernagar, our guest house offers an authentic mountain experience combined with modern comfort and warm hospitality.

We aim to start a modern live music cafe where anyone can come and perform and enjoy the eve.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Himalayan Vista Guest House has been welcoming travelers from around the world to experience the beauty and tranquility of the Himalayas. We have started it as a family-run guest house

Our passion for the mountains and commitment to sustainable tourism drives everything we do. We believe in providing authentic experiences while preserving the natural beauty that surrounds us.
